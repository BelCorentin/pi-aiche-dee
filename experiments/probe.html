Experiment Overview

The Probe experiment uses precisely timed stimuli to isolate specific neural processes in cognitive processing. By carefully controlling the timing of probe stimuli, we can observe how different stages of processing interact and develop over time.

Methodology

In this protocol, participants are presented with a primary task, and at precise time intervals, probe stimuli are introduced to measure the ongoing neural processes. This allows us to create a detailed temporal map of cognitive processing.
