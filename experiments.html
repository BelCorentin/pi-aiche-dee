Research Experiments

This page catalogs the experimental protocols developed during my PhD research. Each experiment includes a description, methodology, and access to sample data and results.

Mindsentences

An experiment examining neural responses to auditory language processing using MEG. This study focuses syntax building and word decoding for controlled and naturalistic stims.

Distraction

This experiment investigates how distractions impact neural dynamics of speech comprehension. Different types of distractions (math, memory tasks etc..) are presented every other normal listening run.

Probe

An experiment where we try to probe both the brain and language models looking for syntactic cues.

Research exploring how preprompting a LLM with some expert qualification would change its embeddings, processing of information and ultimately brain scores.
